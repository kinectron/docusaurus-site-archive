Experiments

Kinectron is currently being used by a community of creative coders at NYU ITP. Here are some of the projects they've created.

Flappy Shadow

Users create and fly a virtual bird from their own shadow puppets. Created by Tong Wu and Kai-che Hung.

Pattern and Sound Series

A series of visual and sonic compositions that reconfigure on live Kinectron input. Created by Katya Rozanova.

How Thrilling

A project that disassociates the body as input from its body as output. Created by Stephanie Koltun.

Future Forest Experience

An immersive future forest experience inspired by a Japanese rock garden. Created by Shawn Ma.
